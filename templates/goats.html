The story of the JTC Goats

Why Goats?

Greatest Of All Time of course. We wanted to align our project with an animal that symbolizes intelligence, creativity, dependability, and calmness.

How to get a Goat?

We gift goats to members of the JTC community who are actively involvd on Discord & Twitter. Custom goats have been given to members of the Jurat Early Adopters Program.
