What are JTC Ordinals and how do they compare with normal Ordinals/NFTs?
JTC Ordinals are tokenized assets part of the JuratBTC blockchain, a Layer 1 Bitcoin protocol with built-in court connectivity. They provide legal rights for creators and collectors of digital art, ensuring enhanced protection for digital assets in the event of scams, hacks or loss of private keys.
How does the Jurat Blockchain offer legal protection for digital art?
JTC Ordinals within the Jurat Blockchain encode chosen licensing terms and royalty requirements into the Ordinals. If someone violates these terms in the future, a judge can issue an injunction to stop it.
